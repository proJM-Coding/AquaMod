The Aqua Buddy from AquaMod

With Aqua Buddy you can sip in style.

The Aqua Buddy is a modular drink bottle which can meet any requirements. Unlike other companies we understand that your drink bottle is not just decoration so we supply something for every use case. This is because we believe that it should be your drink, your way. From fidgets to flavoured straws you can have it all with aqua buddy.

About

AquaMod started up two years ago with the mission to support hydration for children, create healthy habits and encourage users to make and enjoy their personalized experience with a customizable water bottle.

Support both ways

Supporting the community is one of our key values. We support them and they support us.

Features

Every module of the Aqua Buddy is independent from each other allowing you to get your perfect mix.

Effective

Your Aqua Buddy can become any bottle you want. It doesn't matter if it's for a baby, athlete or average person.

Large size

The Aqua Buddy is large enough to keep you hydrated for the full day so you don't need to fill it up.

Eco friendly

We minimize waste with our modules by promoting a bottle which you won't chuck away and unused modules can be sold.

Save Money

The Aqua Buddy is only $15 and can do anything that more expensive do. You only need to buy the modules that you need.
